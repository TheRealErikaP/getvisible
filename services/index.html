Common questions
GOOD questions.
DO I HAVE TO DO A BUILD BEFORE THE MONTHLY PLAN? ▾
Yes — every monthly plan starts with the custom build. This ensures your site is built the right way from day one, with proper SEO structure, fast loading, and a design that converts. The build is a one-time cost ($2,000) and then your monthly plan kicks in from there.
WHAT IF I ALREADY HAVE A WEBSITE I LIKE? ▾
Then the GMB Maximizer is likely the right starting point. It's a $499 one-time service that optimizes your Google Business Profile completely — no build required. If you later want to upgrade to a monthly plan, we can talk about what that looks like for your existing site.
HOW LONG DOES THE BUILD TAKE? ▾
Typically 2–3 weeks from kickoff to launch. This includes a strategy session, design review, and your feedback rounds. I don't rush it — your site needs to be right before it goes live.
CAN I CANCEL MY MONTHLY PLAN? ▾
Yes, monthly plans are month-to-month. I ask for 30 days notice so I can wrap up any in-progress work cleanly. No long contracts, no cancellation fees.
DO YOU WORK WITH CLIENTS OUTSIDE PLACER COUNTY? ▾
Yes — I work with clients in-person in Placer County and remotely with clients anywhere. The process works exactly the same either way. We'll do everything over video call and email if you're not local.
