My story
I BUILT THIS for them.

I'm Erika Poindexter — a Placer County-based marketing professional and licensed Realtor with a deep love for small business owners who are exceptional at their craft.

I kept meeting talented people — seamstresses, music teachers, estheticians, restaurant owners — who couldn't be found online. Not because they weren't good enough. Because no one had helped them show up.

My background is in marketing, sales, and real estate. I understand both the digital side and the small business owner's reality — the long days, the tight budgets, the feeling that marketing is something that happens to other people's businesses, not yours.

I built Visible. to change that. I keep my client list intentionally small. Every business gets real attention, a real strategy, and a real person who picks up the phone. No agency handoffs. No disappearing after launch day. Just me — and a process that works.
